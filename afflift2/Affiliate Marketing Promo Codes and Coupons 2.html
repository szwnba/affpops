How traffic source promo codes work​

The promo codes provided by traffic sources (like PropellerAds for example) will give you a bonus on your next deposit as an advertiser. In some cases, the promo or coupon code will only work for advertisers who have never deposited before or for a new advertiser who is just signing up for the traffic source. In most cases, the promo code will give you a bonus percentage or flat amount for your deposit. An example would be a $50 bonus on a $300 deposit. You get $50 in ad credit for depositing $300 on your account.
