Running Trending Campaigns - Monetizer + PropellerAds


've been testing a bunch of small pop campaigns with PropellerAds and Monetizer using the Trending GEOs on Monetizer and the Traffic Chart on PropellerAds. I'm consistently finding winners so I thought I would share the strategy and maybe you can find some winners too

Part 1 - What's Trending on Monetizer

The first step is of course to have a Monetizer account. If you do not have one, sign up today. Once you have an account, login and check out the Insights section of the menu on the left.

Monetizer gives you access to pretty much all of it's data which is why it's one of my favorite affiliate networks to work with and why it's so effective for this type of strategy.

I typically start with the Trending page because it shows you specifically what GEOs you might want to be checking out.



This table is a comparison of yesterday vs today for each GEO and the network performance of Monetizer. I like to look for GEOs where the rev/epc/leads have all gone up, but the click count has gone down. If the clicks are going down and the revenue is going up, the offers are performing great. You can see with the first row that PY performed really well yesterday, but there was also a huge surge in traffic. Theoretically, this GEO is going to be very competitive right now. KW, on the other hand, saw a 100% increase in revenue but a 9% decrease in clicks. Less competition and better results? Let's give it a try.

IMPORTANT: You can choose whatever GEOs you want and this table will change every single day. What you are seeing might not directly reflect what I am showing above.

Once you've decided on a GEO you want to dig into more, simply click on the GEO (KW #mainstream shown above). Click on Offer and then Clicks.



Now, we have a list of all the top performing offers for KW mainstream traffic from today. We're looking for offers with a high click count and a high eCPM. High click count shows that we have some decent data to work off of and the high eCPM shows that they're converting/earning well. It seems there are a few performing well in this list so that's good.

You can look through the other data available as well besides just the offers. I like to look at the Connection (Monetizer has a lot of carrier billing offers so you want to check the carriers converting), OS, and browser.

Our goal is to setup a campaign on PropellerAds where we're paying a lower average CPM than what the average eCPM is on Monetizer for the GEO. It seems the eCPM for the carrier traffic is $4-8:



I've run enough traffic at PropellerAds to know that the eCPM is high enough to be profitable if I can achieve it because the CPM for KW should be much lower. Let's jump over to PropellerAds chart to see for ourselves (click here to sign up for PropellerAds if you do not already have an advertiser account).

Part 2 - PropellerAds Traffic Chart



The Traffic Chart isn't always 100% accurate so we're going to click to setup a campaign, but it usually gives you some idea of what the traffic will look like. According to the Traffic Chart, there are just over 100k clicks available in KW and their "Optimal CPM Rate" (recommended bid) is $3.44. Considering how close the Optimal CPM Rate is to the Max CPM Rate (highest bid) then I do not believe there is much competition in KW and I will be able to bid lower. Click Create campaign to test it out that theory.

I click to create a campaign for mobile LTE KW traffic and the chart looks pretty promising:



It seems I can bid around $1.60 and get access to most of the traffic. I'll use SmartCPM bidding so I should be bidding lower than $1.60 for most of the ZoneIDs as well.

So, I go back to Monetizer and click to create a new link:



Once you start your campaign you can blacklist or remove any offers that are performing as well as others, but most of the top offers for KW will automatically be rotated into your smartlink since our traffic is going to be targeted to them (KW LTE).

Make sure to add &np=1 to the end of your Monetizer link so you don't utilize their push collection. PropellerAds does not allow push collection on their pop traffic anymore and we're focused on getting conversions not subscribers.

That's it! Finish setting up your campaign in PropellerAds and give them time to add you to the bidding algorithm so you can start getting traffic.

NOT EVERY CAMPAIGN WILL BE A WINNER. MOST CAMPAIGNS WILL BE FAIRLY LOW VOLUME. But, I've been able to string together 5-10 winners consistently on Monetizer and typically with a few offers profitable, it can run for a week or two if not longer. Of course, you can always try to find other offers to test as well to help make the campaigns run longer. For example, for this campaign I could look on MOBIPIUM and Traffic Company to see if they have any KW LTE offers to test. I optimize based on ZoneID and the other variables we already talked about (carrier, browser, etc).
